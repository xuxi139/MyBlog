All u need to know about LUCAS

Lucas Wong, often known simply as Lucas, is a former member of the South Korean boy group NCT, which is managed by SM Entertainment. Born on January 25, 1999, in Hong Kong, Lucas is of Chinese and Thai descent. He gained popularity not only as a member of NCT but also as part of its sub-units NCT U and WayV. Additionally, Lucas was involved in the supergroup SuperM, which consists of members from different SM Entertainment groups.


Early Life and Career:

- Birth Name: Wong Yuk Hei (黃旭熙) - Stage Name: Lucas - Birthplace: Sha Tin, Hong Kong - Nationality: Chinese (Hong Kong) - Ethnicity: Chinese and Thai


Career Highlights:

NCT: Lucas debuted with NCT in 2018, and he was introduced as a part of the rotational unit NCT U. He quickly gained attention for his visuals, deep voice, and energetic stage presence. - WayV: Lucas also debuted as a member of WayV, NCT's China-based unit, which debuted in 2019. - SuperM:Lucas was also selected to join SuperM, a "supergroup" formed by SM Entertainment in 2019, consisting of members from SHINee, EXO, NCT, and WayV. - Modeling and Variety Shows:Apart from his music career, Lucas has also been involved in modeling and appeared on various Korean and Chinese variety shows, further boosting his popularity.
